## The Name

**CXForge** = **CX** (Creative Experience) + **Forge** (Where things are built/crafted)

### Breakdown

| Component | Meaning | Origin |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **CX** | Creative Experience | Evolution from "Cyber Extreme" (2000s) |
| **Forge** | Workshop, foundry, place of creation | Building/crafting metaphor |
| **Solutions** | Professional capability, problem-solving | Business credibility suffix |

---

## The Evolution Story

### Phase 1: Cyber Extreme (Early 2000s)
- Business name in the early 2000s
- Trendy for the era (like ".io" today)
- Gaming and internet culture focused
- Extracted "CX" as the acronym

### Phase 2: cxfos (Gaming & Personal)
- Combined **CX** with **FOS** (Felipe Oliveira dos Santos)
- Used as gaming tag/username across platforms
- Personal brand, always free/available
- Hidden meaning for decades

### Phase 3: CXForge Solutions (2026)
- **CX** = Creative Experience (modern reinterpretation)
- Professional evolution of personal brand
- Maintains connection to origins while scaling
- Meaningful to Latin American and global audiences
